Lunar Phase Calculator

Enter a date and find out the corresponding moon phase!

some notes

If the date is left empty it defaults to the current date.

Entering '0' as year will default to '2000', and entering something like '78' will default to '1978'.

To get the year '78' enter '0078'.

Either '02' and '2' will work for day and month.

Now does it work, yes, but i wouldn't bet my left hand on its accuracy...
