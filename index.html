Lunar Phase Calculator

instructions

Enter a date and find out the moon phase!

  • If the date is left empty it defaults to the current date
  • For single digit months and days you can either enter the single digit or precede it by a 0 (ex.: 02 and 2 are both valid formats for february)
  • Entering 0 as year will default to 2000
  • Entering less than four digits as year will default to the 1900s (78 will be treated as 1978)
  • To get the year 78 enter 0078
  • Enjoy!
